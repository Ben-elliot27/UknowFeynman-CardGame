UNO Flip Game

Instructions

  • To create a new game, click "Create Game" and enter your name.
  • To join an existing game, enter the Game ID and click "Join Game".
  • Once in the game, you can play cards by clicking on them.
  • Click the draw pile to draw a new card.

Flip Mechanic: When a flip card is played, all cards in play are flipped to the opposite side. The flip side has different colors (pink, cyan, orange, purple) and values.
